Reuben’s Deli and Steakhouse
This restaurant is known for its smoked meat sandwiches in Montréal. It is located on St-Catherine Road in downtown Montréal. Many locals love this restaurant and the service is great.

Vin Mon Lapin
Mon Lapin is a well known chic restaurant in Montréal. It is situated in downtown Montréal. It serves small fresh market dishes that are in season. The food is very tasty and the service is quick.

Restaurant Ile Flottante
Ile Flottante is a restaurant located in downtown Montréal. It serves 3 course meals for only 45$. As Well as a 7 course meal for 85$. The restaurant opens at 5pm almost every single day except on Sundays, Mondays and Tuesdays. The restaurant can have a waiting time of 30 minutes if you do not have a reservation because it is quite popular.

Montréal Pool Room
This dine-in and take out restaurant is a popular place in Montréal. It is a quick and affordable fast-food place on main-street. It serves Montréal style hot-dogs and fries and is known for not having any pool table’s.

Schwartz Deli
This restaurant is another one of my favorite places to eat smoked meat in Montréal. It is one of the oldest restaurant place’s in Canada. It is a dine-in, take out and delivery deli. The place serves some of the best sandwiches in my opinion. It’s located on the main street, St-Laurent Road.
