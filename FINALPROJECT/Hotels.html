Sofitel
Sofitel is a popular hotel downtown Montréal. The prices are reasonable at around 178$ per night. It is well located close to the metro station that travels you around the city for really cheap.

Hotel Place d’Armes Old Montréal
This hotel is on the more expensive side. It is around 300$ per night. The hotel is a 3 minute walk from the Palais des Congres. It is located downtown and close to a metro station. It is an older building in Montréeal. It has great service and staff.
