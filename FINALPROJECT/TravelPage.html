Montréal, Québec
Montréal is a beautiful city. It is the largest city in Québec with a population of 1.78 million. Montréal has welcomed more than 10.2 million overnight visitors in 2016. It also had over 11,792,970 day trip tourists in 2010. There are many tourist attractions in Montréal, like the View-Port of Montréal, the Notre-Dame Basilica of Montréal, The Montréal Museum of Fine Arts and The Mont-Royal.
